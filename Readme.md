*SaaS Churn & Retention Analytics Project Overview* 

Customer retention is one of the most critical success factors for any SaaS business. As subscription-based companies scale, increasing workflow complexity, product failures, support issues, and pricing friction can negatively impact customer satisfaction and lead to customer churn.

This project simulates a real-world SaaS environment to analyze customer behavior and identify the key factors influencing churn. Using SQL, Excel, and Power BI, the project transforms raw customer data into actionable business insights that support retention-focused decision making.

Business Problem 

SaaS companies often experience customer churn due to a combination of product reliability issues, support inefficiencies, and pricing-related friction. Without a structured analytics framework, it becomes difficult to identify at-risk customers and understand the underlying reasons for customer attrition.

This project aims to analyze customer behavior, product usage, workflow failures, and support interactions to identify churn drivers and recommend data-driven retention strategies.

Objectives Analyze customer churn behavior across different subscription plans. Identify relationships between workflow failures, support interactions, and customer satisfaction. Measure key SaaS metrics such as Churn Rate, Retention Rate, and Customer Lifetime Value (LTV). Segment customers based on subscription plans and usage patterns. Deliver business recommendations to improve customer retention. Tools Used SQL Microsoft Excel Power BI Dataset 

A synthetic SaaS dataset was designed to simulate realistic customer behavior across multiple subscription tiers. The dataset includes customer information, subscription details, workflow usage, automation activity, support interactions, customer satisfaction, and churn indicators.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) Churn Rate Retention Rate Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) Customer Satisfaction Score Support Ticket Volume Workflow Failure Rate Business Questions Which subscription plans experience the highest churn? How do workflow failures influence customer retention? Does customer satisfaction impact churn? Which customer segments generate the highest revenue? What are the major reasons customers contact support? Which users should be considered high-risk for churn? Dashboard 

The Power BI dashboard provides interactive insights into:

Customer segmentation Churn trends Revenue distribution Product usage Support performance Customer satisfaction Workflow reliability Key Insights 

Insights generated during the analysis will be documented after completing SQL analysis and dashboard development.

Business Recommendations 

Recommendations will focus on reducing churn by improving product reliability, optimizing customer support, and identifying high-risk customer segments through data-driven analysis.
